Every Age, Every Stage

We understand that every child develops at their own pace. Our age-specific programs are designed to meet children where they are, building confidence and skills through play-based learning.

From gentle water introduction for infants to stroke development for preschoolers, each lesson is tailored to your child's developmental stage and comfort level.

Safety is our top priority, with certified instructors and small class sizes ensuring personalized attention.

6–18 Months

Infant Splash Program

  • Water confidence building
  • Parent-child bonding techniques
  • Gentle submersion introduction

18 Months – 3 Years

Toddler Swim Program

  • Breath control and floating skills
  • Independent movement in water
  • Water safety awareness

3–5 Years

Preschool Strokes Program

  • Basic stroke technique development
  • Underwater swimming confidence
  • Pool safety and rescue skills
